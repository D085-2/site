# Arnold Air Society

## About
We are the Doolittle Squadron. We are named in honor of General James "Jimmy" Doolittle, who was a pioneer in aviation and the leader of the Doolittle Raid during WWII.

## Activities

We hold our squadron meetings every Friday at 1800 (6:00 PM). Each of our departments leads initiatives such as campus clean-ups, local volunteering, and fundraising for national causes. Every year, we attend the National Conclave (NATCON). It's a gathering where AAS and Silver Wings chapters from across the country meet to network, attend professional workshops, and vote on national policy and leadership.
## Contact

Arnold Air Society membership is earned through our candidacy program, which is 6 weeks long. The program is designed to test candidates and prepare them for Air Force Leadership. We challenge them on critical thinking and problem-solving, drill, leadership and teamwork, AAS/ Air Force Heritage, and knowledge
