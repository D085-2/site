# Honor Guard

## About
Honor Guard is a professional ceremonial unit representing all Airmen worldwide. With a focus on heritage, perfecting image, and protecting standards, the team focuses on flag ceremonies and other similar events throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

- [Uniforms](https://static.e-publishing.af.mil/production/1/af_a1/publication/dafi36-2903/dafi36-2903.pdf)

- [Army Honor Guard](https://www.usarcent.army.mil/Portals/1/Documents/regs/TC%203-21.5%20Drill%20and%20Ceremonies%20May%202021.pdf?ver=H1VhEGl3Y14hZR6D2vsBRA%3D%3D)
- [Honor Guard Website](https://www.honorguard.af.mil/)

## Activities
Throughout a typical year, the Detachment 085 Honor Guard follows the academic school year for activities and events. Primarily focusing on football, baseball, basketball, and other sports to honor the nation before each sporting event.

## Contact
To join the Detachment 085 Honor Guard you must simply be an actively enrolled ROTC cadet. Attend the first meeting of the semester or reach out to the active Honor Guard commander for more information.
