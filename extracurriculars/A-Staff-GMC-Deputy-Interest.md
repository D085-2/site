# A-Staff GMC Deputy Interest

This form is to gauge the interest GMC may have in becoming GMC deputies for certain A-Staff directorates. Note that you may not get the first choices you want.

## Description of the A-Staff Directorates

### Directorate of Personnel & Admin Support (A-1)

Responsible for tracking attendance, test scores, and performance documentation. Ensures records are accurate and consistent, coordinates attendance with other directorates, and manages assignments such as flag duty and cleaning schedules.

### Directorate of Logistics (A-4)

Oversees the movement and management of supplies, equipment, and materials essential for LLAB, events, and oversees the Uniform Monitor team. Coordinates logistics requests, ensures timely delivery of resources, prints OPORDs/CONOPs, and keeps Flight Commander iPads updated with necessary documents.

### Directorate of Communications & Public Affairs (A-6)

Handles public affairs, social media, and community engagement. Manages the detachment calendar, runs ROTC social channels (e.g., Instagram), maintains ROTC's image through photos and flyers, and coordinates outreach campaigns with other directorates.

### Directorate of Strategic Planning & Special Events (A-8)

Plans and executes detachment events, morale and wellness activities, and large-scale programs like FTX, Cal Day, and fundraisers. Coordinates with A-6 for outreach, manages MWR events, and ensures unit participation in community service projects.

### Directorate of Studies & Analyses, Assessments and Lessons Learned (A-9)

Focuses on assessment, process improvement, and documentation management. Collects and analyzes academic and attendance data, maintains cadet information systems (SharePoint, Slack, Google Drive, website), and advises leadership on training priorities and needed improvements.
