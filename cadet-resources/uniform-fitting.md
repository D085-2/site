# Uniform Fitting

Book your uniform fitting appointment below. Pick any open slot, and you'll receive a confirmation email and a reminder before your appointment. If you need to reschedule or cancel, use the link in that email. Once you're booked, add it to your phone's calendar and set an alert so you don't miss it.
